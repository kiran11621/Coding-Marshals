How to Make Contest?

   1

Create Contest

Go to Create Contest. Fill the Form. Give Correct Username of the Contest Creator (The Username should be Verified i.e. use the same as Registered), Date and Time for organizing the Contest. As, once the form is submitted the details can't be modify/change by User.

2   

Add Question

You have Two Choices:

1. Add Question from already prepared Question list provided by Code Marshals

2. Else, Prepare your own Question

   3

Question View

You will get the view of all the questions added to the contest.

Note: You will be not allowed to make any changes after you click submit. So, make sure you are sure with your choices.

4   

Share Passcode

After creating a Contest you will get a mail from CodeMarshals176@gmail.com. Having details for Contest and Passcode. You can share the Passcode with your Friends and ask them to join the Contest through Ongoing Contest List. You can control who can take part in the Contest. It's Amazing.

How to Make Questions?

  • 01

    Question Information


    Give a proper meaningful Question Name, give a valid Author Username i.e. registered (Author Username is same as User's Username) and provide your problem statement of your question.

    •         

  • 02

    Input, Output, Tags and Constraints


    Give Proper information about Input Format and Output Format so no ambiguity arise at User's side during solving. Provide valid Constraints and Tags. As, Tags can be used for Question filtering.

    •         

  • 03

    Hidden Input and Hidden Output


    Hidden Input and Hidden Output is to be provided in '.txt' file. The content of these file will not be displayed to user during contest. It increases the competitiveness of the Question.

    •         

  • 04

    Submit


    You have two choices:

    1. Submit and Create Another.

    2. Else, Submit and Go to Question List where you can search for your Question and add to the Contest.
